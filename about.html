The Heart of Junnar

A Legacy of Hospitality

Where Comfort Meets History

Established in the historic shadow of Shivneri Fort, Hotel Aashiyana was founded on a simple principle: to offer a "home away from home" for travelers. We cater to every need, from quiet family stays to grand celebrations.

"Our mission is to preserve traditional hospitality while providing the modern facilities required for today's events and stays."
